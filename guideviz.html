Guideviz

A guidance system for the visually impaired

"Guideviz" is a mobile application designed to enhance the lives of individuals with visual impairments. By providing essential support in navigation and communication, it empowers them to overcome challenges and lead more independent and fulfilling lives.

Experience simplicity at your fingertips! With a single tap, the app narrates each button's purpose with voice, ensuring effortless navigation. Need to dive deeper? Long-press any button to seamlessly access the features. Your journey, made smoother than ever before!

Our app utilizes the Google Maps API , streamlining navigation for the visually impaired. Location input is voice-enabled, minimizing clicks. A tap offers voice guidance, while a long press unlocks further options. Simple, accessible, and empowering.

Powered by Flutter

Powered by Flutter, our app offers a seamless experience for the visually impaired. With its intuitive interface and efficient functionalities, it redefines accessibility, making navigation simpler and more intuitive.
